Quiz

Answer the following questions as best as you can, and the quiz will make recommendations on managing your mental health!

Question 1

How social are you?

Fairly social; I love talking with people and I am not generally nervous conversing, even with strangers
Somewhat social; I'll talk when other people are talking but I don't generally start conversations unless I'm with my close ones
Not very social; I'm pretty shy and generally keep to myself in most situations, but I occasionally participate in social activities
Not social at all; I strongly prefer being alone and very rarely converse or participate in social activities

Question 2

How physically active are you?

Very active; I frequently exercise by either playing sports or working out at a gym
Somewhat active; I often go on walks or runs, and I'll participate in more intense activity on occasion
Not very active; I'm not generally physically active but I am not completely opposed to it
Not active at all; I don't like exerting my body and exclusively stay indoors

Question 3

On average, how many hours do you sleep per night?

8+ hours
6-8 hours
1-6 hours
Less than 1 hour

Question 4

How open are you to discussing your mental wellbeing?

Very open; I make sure to talk about things that are weighing me down. I don't have an issue seeking professional help when/if I may need it.
Somewhat open; I do talk about my issues with people that I am close with, but not on a regular basis
Not generally open; it's hard to open up to others but I have been able to talk about the bigger things that weigh me down the most
Not open at all; I get extremely uncomfortable discussing what's on my mind with other people, even if I am close with them

Question 5

Rate your mental health

4/4 -- I feel great 24/7!
3/4 -- Sometimes I feel down, but I'm generally happy
2/4 -- Not generally the happiest, but not generally sad
1/4 -- I feel depressed and/or anxious most days, and I would like to receive help
