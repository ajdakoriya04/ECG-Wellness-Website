Activities

Below is a list of activities that can help lower stress and improve focus.

Physical Activity

Whether it's simply walking around, dancing, or a full game of basketball, any kind of physical activity will do wonders for your mental health and physical fitness by helping calm the mind and keep your heart rate up. Studies show a link between positive mental health and physical activity, among other healthy habits.

via GIPHY


Yoga

Yoga and meditation emphasize focus and positive emotions. Such activity is even known to help with awareness, memory, and attention, according to Harvard Health. You can find yoga excercises here: https://www.verywellfit.com/essential-yoga-poses-for-beginners-3566747.

via GIPHY


Sleep and Nutrition

Like with physical activity, it is imperative to sleep and eat right for our mental and physical health. Getting at least 8 hours of sleep and eating enough fruits and vegetables helps maintain energy and focus during the day, correlating with positive mental health (Source).

Socializing

Humans are social animals. Mayo Clinic recommends socializiation, as it can help us prevent feeling lonely in addition to benefitting memory, happiness, and (potentially) lifespan.
